Just straight up nutrition on a mission

Whether you’re an athlete or committed to an active lifestyle, our mission is to empower healthy living by bringing together all the best superfoods into a convenient, wholesome, and well-balanced meal — that’s good for you, and the planet. 

Our values

Honesty

Planet-Friendly

Convenience

Our Founders

Darren O'Reilly

Ex-professional athlete

After 6 years as a professional rugby player with access to the best nutritionists, I left the sports fields for the corporate world.

I soon struggled to balance a busy lifestyle with essential nutritional needs. I wanted a healthy solution that tasted great too, and so I came up with Whole Supp: a delicious, nutritionally complete meal that’s good for both you and the planet.

Dr Brian Carson

Head of Science & Innovation

As our Head of Science and Innovation, I came up with Whole Supp’s Smart Food formulation and lead our scientific research studies.

I studied human metabolism, nutrition and exercise for 15 years and hold a PhD in physiology. The formulation consistent of 13 plant-based ingredients, including superfoods to support both performance and recovery.
